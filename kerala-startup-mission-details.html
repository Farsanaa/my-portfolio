Event Overview

Part of Women Makers Celebration I had the wonderful opportunity to attend the Women Makers Celebration 2026 on March 14 at Kerala Startup Mission, Kalamassery, Kochi. Being selected among the Top 300 Makers of Tink Her Hack 4.0 made this experience even more special. It was truly inspiring to be surrounded by talented makers, innovators, and mentors who are passionate about building and creating. The event was a great platform to connect, learn, and celebrate the spirit of women in technology and innovation. I'm really grateful for this opportunity and the experience will definitely stay with me as motivation to keep learning and building. Thank you to the organizers TinkerHub Foundation, Samagata Foundation, and Kerala Startup Mission for making this celebration possible.
